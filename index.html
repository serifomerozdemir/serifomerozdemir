Target Audience:

  • Anyone who owns any of these products:
    Computer , Tablet, Mobile Phone

Reasons:

  • These devices, whose main purpose is video playback, are now one of the indispensable tools of our age. These devices, which enable to play videos in different formats, can also include multimedia features such as connecting to the internet, connecting external disks, playing music files, playing games, showing photos and downloading files.

Usage Patterns:

  • You can use our application on MacOS, Windows, Android, HarmonyOS operating systems on computers, tablets and mobile phones

Benefits :

  • -We can play it as audio only.
  • -We can increase or decrease the playback speed.
  • -We can change the voice type (like a person swallowing helium gas).

  • -By opening it in a small playback window, we can watch the video in the small window and on the other side, we can do our work from the relevant platform (such as browsing social media, looking at pictures).
